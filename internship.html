Internship at Workiva

Internship on the Data Visualization team.

I was hired on as an intern at Workiva in the summer of 2015. I was then extended for the school year and ended up working there for a whole year. I was a member of the data visualization team.

I was treated just like a full time developper; only with the understanding that I have less availability and will most likely not work as fast as the other teammates. As such, I participated in their development sprints and picked up tickets to work on on my own.

Our product was the data visualization component of Workiva's software. Thus, I worked in front-end development using Dart (a language Google developped, and which compiles to Javascript), HTML, and CSS. This internship vastly improved my web-development skills and taught me how to function in an agile development team using Git for code management.
