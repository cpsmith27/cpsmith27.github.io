ENG 314 - Final Project

The Calculator

This was a technical communications course. During the semester, we worked to improve our technical communication so that we may be more effective workers in industry.

During the semester, we studied design principles such as contrast, repetition, alignment and position; and how these principles affect technical documents. We had several projects. These were a document redesign, the creation of technical instructions, and an infographic.

For our final project, we were each to create a portfolio of our showcasing what we had learnt during the semester. I decided to create a website myself, challenging myself to improve my web design skills.
